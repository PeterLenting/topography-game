About the Game

  • Every card shows a professional (ex-)cyclist with a recognisable haircut and the shirt in which he was most successful.
  • The lower part of the card contains the score of the rider on six important qualities on a scale from 0-100. Next to his head are the big prices he won.
  • The pink jersey represents a Giro-win, the yellow one a Tour-win, the red one a Vuelta-win and the Roman pillar a win in a Monument.

How to Play?

  • Check the jersey, qualities, races won and haircut of the rider
  • Do you know who it is?
  • Enter his name (first- and lastname) and hit submit
  • If you're right, you get 15 points
  • Not sure? Ask for a hint and the flag of his nation is shown
  • A hint will cost you 5 point, just like a wrong answer does
  • After two wrong answers, the right answer will be shown. So use the hint wisely
  • Each game contains 10 riders
  • Don't doubt too long, every second you have to spare is added to your score
  • Enjoy!
